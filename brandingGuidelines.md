# 10. Component Styling Rules

Cards:

* White background
* 24dp corner radius
* Soft shadow
* Internal padding: 16dp

Background:

* Always gradient-based
* Never plain flat color

Text over gradient:

* Always white or near-white
* Add slight drop shadow if needed

---
